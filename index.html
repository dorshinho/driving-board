Take a Drive

Driving Board is a mobile app and social platform which helps you to plan road trips with ease. The app is fully loaded with cutting edge features to help you collaborate with like minded individuals to plan and execute travel safely and successfully.

Travel without obstacles

When it comes to travelling, we believe nothing should stop you. Driving board was designed to get people back out on the 'open road' and visiting new places. Road trips are a great, cheap way to explore your surroundings and make new experiences.

Create lasting memories

We have implemented a state-of-the art map tracking system into our app to assure safe travel.

We believe that saftey is one of the most important things when it comes to travelling. Whether getting on the road with friends or new acquaintances, Rest assusred that we’ve got your back!
